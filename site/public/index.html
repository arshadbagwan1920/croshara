Size Guide

Find the Perfect Fit

Getting the right size is easy. Follow these simple steps to measure your baby's feet.

1

Trace the Foot

Place your baby's foot on a piece of paper and trace around it. Keep the pencil straight!

2

Measure Length

Measure from heel to longest toe in centimetres. Do both feet — one is usually slightly larger.

3

Match Our Chart

Use the chart below to find the perfect size. Our wool stretches slightly for a custom fit.

SizeAgeFoot Length
XS0–3 monthsUp to 8 cm
S3–6 months8–9.5 cm
M6–9 months9.5–11 cm
L9–12 months11–12.5 cm
XL12–18 months12.5–13.5 cm
XXL18–24 months13.5–15 cm

💡 Wool stretches — when in doubt, size down for a snug fit.

FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about CROSHARA booties.

What makes CROSHARA different from other baby shoes? +
We use 100% natural wool (not cotton blends or acrylic), our booties are barefoot-friendly with flexible soles, and each pair is handcrafted by skilled Indian artisans. Most mass-market brands use synthetic materials and stiff soles.
Are the booties safe for newborns? +
Absolutely. Our wool is naturally hypoallergenic, chemical-free, and incredibly soft. The flexible sole supports natural foot development. Always supervise your baby while wearing any footwear.
How do I clean the booties? +
Hand wash in cold water with mild detergent. Gently squeeze (don't twist), reshape, and air dry away from direct sunlight. They'll be good as new!
What sizes do you offer? +
We cover 0-24 months with 6 sizes: XS (0-3mo), S (3-6mo), M (6-9mo), L (9-12mo), XL (12-18mo), and XXL (18-24mo). See our size guide above for exact measurements.
Do you ship internationally? +
Currently we ship across India with free shipping. International shipping coming soon — follow us on Instagram @crosharahandmade for updates!
Can I customize an order? +
Yes! We accept custom orders for color combinations, embellishments, and gift messages. Minimum 5 pairs for custom batches. DM us on Instagram to discuss your ideas.
What is your return policy? +
We want you to love your purchase. If there's a defect or wrong size, contact us within 7 days and we'll make it right. Since each pair is handmade, minor variations in size (±0.5 cm) are normal and add to the charm.
How long does delivery take? +
Orders are dispatched within 2-3 business days. Delivery takes 3-7 days depending on your location. You'll receive tracking details once shipped.
