# CROSHARA Daily Automation Workflow

## Schedule (IST)

### 6:00 AM - AI Content Generation
Use ChatGPT/Gemini (free) with saved prompts:
1. Generate 1 Reel script + caption + hashtags
2. Generate 5 Pinterest pin descriptions
3. Generate 1 YouTube Short script
4. Generate 1 WhatsApp broadcast message

**Prompt to use:**
```
Create a [Reel/Pin/Short] for CROSHARA handmade baby shoes brand. 
Product focus: [product name]
Target audience: Indian parents of babies 0-3 years
Tone: Warm, nurturing, proud Indian craftsmanship
Include: Hook, body, CTA, hashtags
```

### 7:00 AM - Canva Content Creation
1. Open Canva template for today's format
2. Swap photos and text with AI-generated content
3. Download as MP4 (Reel) or PNG (Pin)
4. Save to dated folder

### 9:00 AM - Pinterest Scheduling
Schedule 5 pins using Metricool free tier:
- 2 product pins
- 1 educational pin
- 1 curated pin
- 1 CROSHARA pin

### 10:00 AM - Instagram Stories
Post 3-5 Stories:
- 1 product/story
- 1 poll or question
- 1 behind-the-scenes or quote
- 1 testimonial
- 1 CTA (link/DM)

### 11:00 AM - WhatsApp Broadcast
Send daily broadcast:
- Monday: Product showcase
- Tuesday: Baby care tips
- Wednesday: Behind-the-scenes
- Thursday: Customer testimonial
- Friday: Offer/discount
- Saturday: Weekend special
- Sunday: Rest (or engagement post)

### 2:00 PM - Instagram Post
Publish today's main post (Reel or Carousel)
Engage with comments for 15 mins

### 4:00 PM - Engagement Window
- Like 30 competitor posts
- Comment on 15 baby niche posts
- Follow 50 target accounts (parents, baby pages)
- Reply to all DMs and comments

### 6:00 PM - Analytics Check (Daily)
Log metrics to Google Sheets:
- Instagram: followers, reach, engagement, saves
- WhatsApp: messages sent, replies, orders
- Pinterest: impressions, saves, clicks

### 8:00 PM - YouTube Publishing (if scheduled)
Upload Short if it's Sunday
Optimize: title, description, tags, thumbnail

### 9:00 PM - Daily Wrap
- Update sales tracker
- Log successful patterns to Ruflo memory
- Plan tomorrow's content
- Check tomorrow's scheduled posts

---

## Weekly Tasks (Sunday)

### Content Planning (30 mins)
- Plan next week's 7 Reels
- Create Pinterest pins for the week (batch)
- Prepare WhatsApp broadcast schedule
- Review analytics from previous week

### Admin (15 mins)
- Process pending orders
- Update inventory
- Respond to all pending messages
- Review finances
