Behind the scenes: How CROSHARA booties are made 🧶✨

From skein to shoe — every pair goes through:
1️⃣ Wool selection (only the softest)
2️⃣ Hand-crocheting (4+ hours per pair)
3️⃣ Quality check (every stitch inspected)
4️⃣ Gentle wash & block
5️⃣ Gift wrapping with love

No factories. No machines. Just pure skill and patience.

This is what handmade truly means. 🇮🇳🧶
