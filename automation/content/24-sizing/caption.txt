👣 Finding the perfect fit — easier than you think!

How to size your baby for CROSHARA booties:

1️⃣ Trace foot on paper
2️⃣ Measure heel-to-longest-toe in cm
3️⃣ Check our size guide (link in bio)

Size chart:
👶 0-6mo → foot up to 9cm (Newborn)
👶 6-12mo → foot up to 11cm (Infant)
👶 12-18mo → foot up to 12.5cm (Walker)
👶 18-24mo → foot up to 14cm (Toddler)

Pro tip: Wool stretches! If between sizes, size down for a snug fit.
