How to care for your CROSHARA wool booties 🧶

1️⃣ Hand wash with mild soap
2️⃣ Cold water only (no hot!)
3️⃣ Gently squeeze (don't wring)
4️⃣ Air dry flat (away from sunlight)
5️⃣ Store in a dry place

With proper care, your booties last for months — and can even be passed down! 👶➡️👶
